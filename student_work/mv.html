Cat Breeds
Cornish Rex They have short, yet extremely soft hair, as well as large ears. They are very playful and enegetic too.
Sphynx They are often refered to as a hairless cat, since they have no fur. They are very active and seek a lot of attention. They are also very vocal.
Bombay They are medium sized with a short coat. They are very playful and fairly active. They ae usually black, too.

Maine Coon

This is a type of cat called a Maine Coon.
They are a larger type of cat and have long hair.
They are very playful and fairly energetic.

Ocicat

Ocicats very closely resemble an ocelot,
but have no shared DNA with them.
Ocicats are both extremely playful and energetic.
They are very vocal and seek attention.
