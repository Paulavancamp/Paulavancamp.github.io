About BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed by BigHit Enterainment. They debuted on June 12, 2013 with the song "No More Dream" from their first album 2 Cool 4 Skool.

RM


The members' names may seem hard to remember, so I will help you out! The first member is RM, also known as Kim Namjoon(or Joonie). He is the leader and spokesperson for the group and is one of three rappers in the group.

Jin


The next member is Jin, also known as Kim Seokjin. He is the oldest of the seven, and is a vocalist. He loves to tell cringey dad jokes to the other member and his laugh is contagious. You may recognize his handsome face from when he turned twitter famous as "car door guy".

Suga


Next, we have Suga, also known as Min Yoongi. He, too, is a rapper like RM. Suga was also known as Agust D, the stage name "Suga" is Agus spelled backwards(Just add the T and D!) J-hope


J-Hope, also known as Jung Hoseok, is the main dancer of the group and part of the rap line along with Suga and RM. He usually learns the choreography first and teaches the other members.
