Aiderondak Chair Construction: This project was thought of by my friend Mary one day when we were bored. Life happened though and we ended up leaving all the materials we bought in my dad's shop for a year without making any progress. But after those 12 months, I picked it up again with my (then)boyfriend. This partnership too did not produce any quick results however we did end up constructing a useable chair. I am proud of this project because I learned that I actually have a deep love for carpentry... and that my ex-boyfriend does not.

My first creation was a Frank Lloyd Wright inspired dinning room chair. My Dad was large part of the process. I'm definitely most proud of this one

This summer at work (American Eagle) we moved locations and had to throw out a ton of furniture so I grabbed this beautiful table to bring home. This commercial sized table is a little too tall for regular chairs and a little too short for bar stools so this motivated me to start on my next project! I made a set of 4 custom height stools for this table. Only took me about a week working on it here and there. They look great!
