ALL ABOUT ME

Paula Van Camp

Paula is an engineer and environmentalist.
She driven to create a world in which the inherent value of all things is understood and respected.
Paula loves to create and refurbish furniture, to ride her bike, and make gadgets for her many plants. She is a spirited student at Marquette University and Co-Founder of REVIVE. You will find her anywhere the sun in shining.

GIRLS WHO CODE

I am the level 2 instructor for Marquette University's Girls Who Code club. In Level 2, we learn HTML, CSS, and fundamentals of JavaScript. These skills, and insights about best coding practices, provide the necessary education needed to create beautiful websites.
Each student works hard to understand all three languages and applies their understanding to their final project in which they design and implement a website of their own. This year's class projects can be viewed below.

REVIVE

Revive is a plastic recycling workspace located on Marquette's campus. Myself and co-founder Colleen Dunlap have been working hard to launch the program and create a closed loop recycling system that is open to all! More to come :)
